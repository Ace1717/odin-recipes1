Spaghetti

Description

Traditional spaghetti with meat sauce. It has ground beef in the tomato sauce.

Igredients

  • Pound ground beef
  • A medium onion
  • 4 clove garlic
  • 1 can tomato sauce
  • 1 can diced tomatoes

Steps

  1. Gather ingredients
  2. combine ground beef and garlic in sauce pan. Put on medium heat. Cook and stir until meat is brown.
  3. Stir diced tomatoes and sauce into the pan.
  4. Mix all the ingredients and enjoy!
