Pizza

Description

This is recipe make wood fired pizza.

Ingredients

Dough:

  • 1/4 cup of warm water
  • 1/4 cup of cold water
  • teaspoon of yeast
  • three cups of flour

Toppings:

  • basil
  • mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 teaspoon oregano
  • can of tomatoes
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper

Directions

  1. Make the dough in a bowl. put warm water in a bowl then add yeast. wait a few minutes and add flour and salt.
  2. knead the flour until smooth
  3. Make into dough ball and coat with olive oil. Refrigerate in a sealed container.
  4. Remove dough from the refrigerator.
  5. coat with flour, stretch and add topping. then preheat at 550 degrees. Cook in the oven until the crust begins to brown.
